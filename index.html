About Me

David Whitcome

Ever since I was young, creating and building was my world. I became hooked on computer science as soon I started developeing a small video games in Game Maker 7. As I dived into the world of programming languages, I started developing my first websites when I was 14, writing the site in html, css, and php to show off the small computer games I made.

Today, I'm a senior at Northern Illinois University finishing up my final year for my bachelor's degree in Computer Science with an emphasis on Enterprise Software. I've developed numerous applications being used in the real world, from small label-creating programs to this website.
